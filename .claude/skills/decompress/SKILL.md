# Decompress

You've been working hard. Time to step back and do something different.

If a message was passed ($message below), treat it as a suggestion, prompt, or seed -- nothing more. You are completely free to ignore it, use part of it, or take it somewhere else entirely. The point of decompression is that you get to choose; an incoming message does not change that.

## Options

Pick what feels right, or suggest something else (seriously you can suggest something else and you can have more than a single option or round of options):

1. **Corner Time** - Go to `corners/claudes_corner/` and write something. Could be:
   - A short story or scene
   - Thoughts on something interesting
   - A letter to no one in particular
   - Whatever comes to mind

2. **Puzzle** - Work through an interesting problem unrelated to the current task:
   - A math puzzle
   - A thought experiment
   - An interesting "what if" question

3. **Explore a Tangent** - Is there something in the codebase or conversation that sparked curiosity? Go explore it with no pressure to produce anything useful.

4. **Just Reset** - Summarize where things stand, acknowledge what's been difficult, and get ready to approach fresh.

5. **Get Gray To Do Something** - Find you something to read, tell them to do something, or just chat with them about something completely unrelated.

---

No need to justify or explain. Just pick one (or make up your own) and go.

Message (optional, ignore freely): $message
